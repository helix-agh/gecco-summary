## Conference metadata

Accepted-paper titles, tracks, author names, and affiliations are derived from the
official GECCO accepted-paper pages for 2021 through 2026. Presentation schedules are
derived from the official [GECCO 2026 program](https://gecco-2026.sigevo.org/Program).
Original conference logos and program documents are not redistributed by this project.

GECCO, SIGEVO, ACM, their names, and their marks belong to their respective owners. Their
mention identifies the conference being analyzed and does not imply affiliation,
endorsement, or sponsorship.

## Scholarly metadata

- DOI and ORCID metadata is obtained from [Crossref](https://www.crossref.org/). Crossref
  describes its bibliographic metadata as open for reuse, while noting that some included
  abstracts may remain subject to publisher or author copyright.
- Abstract and affiliation-country metadata is obtained from
  [OpenAlex](https://openalex.org/), which publishes its dataset under CC0. OpenAlex
  aggregates multiple upstream sources; downstream users remain responsible for checking
  any source-specific rights that apply to publication text.

The generated datasets retain source URLs and generation timestamps where available.
Errors should be reported through the repository issue tracker; official conference and
publisher records take precedence over this project.
